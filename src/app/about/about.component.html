About

Ultra Sports India (USI) started out in 2017 with an aim of developing football ecosystem in India. USI has been at the forefront of football development and a market leader with its football schools and coach education programs, creating a community around football. One of the fastest growing Sports Management company in India, USI with properties like Football Forward has introduced global coach education programs to India, and revolutionized the same.
USI’s Soccer Schools have been Thane’s biggest soccer school program in terms of student participation.
