Thane City Football Club

Thane City FC is a club based in the city of Thane. Started in 2018, TCFC aims to be one of the first pro-teams from the city to play at the national level. The TCFC Soccer Schools train more than 400 children in Thane.

Football Forward

Football Forward is a Coach-Education program where industry experts from around the world come to interact with coaches and have knowledge-sharing workshops to empower Indian coaches.
